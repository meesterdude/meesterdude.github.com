My Top ArduinoVersusEvil Videos

Mar 9th, 2018 • ∞

Very knowledgeable and always entertaining, ArduinoVersusEvil is a canadian based youtuber with extensive understanding of electrical and mechanical engineering. His channel is primarily taking things apart, or working on his own projects. I’ve watched a lot of them, so I thought I’d highlight some of my favorites!
